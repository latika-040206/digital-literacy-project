cybercrime Type: UPI/Online payment Fraud


UPI fraud is a common cybercrime where scammers trick people into sending money through digital payment apps like Google pay,Phone Pay or paytm.
This type of fraud is increasing in india as more people use online payment system for transactions.
This fraud can usually happen in many ways.
first,the scammer contacts the victim through calls,social media or messaging apps
second,they create situation such as refund,reward or offers payment.
third,they send a fake UPI request or link to convince the victim.
students,online sellers and people who frequently use digital payment system are mostly targeted.
The consequence include financial loss,panic and misuse of personal information.
Incidents like this show why users must be careful while approving and UPI transactions
A common incident involves a college student who have been victim of such UPI fraud.
He received the scammer's call pretending to be from the bank and asked for OTP and he being careless shared the OTP resulting in financial loss
