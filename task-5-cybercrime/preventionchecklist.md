#stay safe online - cybercrime preventon checklist


-Always verify unknown calls,messages or emails before responding.
-Never share your OTP,passwords,bank details,or UPI PIN with anyone.
-Do not click suspicious links sent through SMS,email or social media.
-use strong and unique passwords for your accounts.
-Enable two-factor authentication whenever possible.
-Rgeularly update your apps,phone software and antivirus protection.
-Avoid using pubic wi-fi for banking entering personal information.
-check website URLs carefully before entering personal informations.

#UPI/Financial safety tips


-Remember: To receive money on UPI, you do not need to enter your UPI pin
-Always double-check the name of the person before approving a payment request
